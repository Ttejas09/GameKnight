UNDERTALE

UNDERTALE

  • OVERVIEW

Game: Undertale

  • Developer: Toby Fox.
  • Release Year: 2015
  • Genre: Role-Playing Game (RPG)
  • Platform: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch

Created by a Single Developer

Undertale was mostly created by Toby Fox alone. He handled the coding, story, characters, and music himself, making it a rare example of a successful solo-developed game.

You Don’t Have to Kill Anyone

Unlike most RPGs, Undertale allows you to complete the entire game without killing any enemies. You can choose to talk, spare, or befriend them instead of fighting.

Your Choices Change Everything

The game has multiple endings based on your actions: Pacifist Route (no killing) Neutral Route Genocide Route (eliminate everyone) Each route completely changes the story and characters.

Characters Remember Your Actions

The game remembers what you did, even after restarting. Characters may react differently based on your past choices, breaking the usual “reset everything” rule in games.

Famous Characters

Undertale features iconic characters like: Sans – a lazy skeleton with hidden power Papyrus – his energetic brother Toriel – a caring mother figure Flowey – a mysterious and dangerous flower
