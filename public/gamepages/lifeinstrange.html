LIFE IS STRANGE

OVERVIEW

Life is Strange is an intimate story about two estranged friends reuniting. As Max, you discover you can rewind time while investigating the mysterious disappearance of a fellow student. Together with your best friend Chloe, uncover the darker underbelly of your picturesque hometown. This narrative-driven adventure emphasizes player choice and consequence, where every decision shapes the story. Experience a beautiful blend of indie storytelling, photography, and the supernatural.

KEY FEATURES

PLOT

Max Caulfield hasn't seen her childhood best friend Chloe in five years. When their paths cross again in their hometown of Arcadia Bay, they reconnect in a small bathroom at the local coffee shop. Max reveals she has discovered an incredible power: the ability to rewind time. While investigating the mysterious disappearance of fellow student Rachel Amber, Max and Chloe uncover a dangerous secret that threatens their town. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they must confront supernatural forces, dark conspiracies, and moral dilemmas that will test their friendship and change them forever.
