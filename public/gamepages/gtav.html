GRAND THEFT AUTO V

OVERVIEW

Grand Theft Auto V is a sprawling action-adventure game set in the fictional metropolis of Los Santos. Take control of three unique protagonists—Michael, Trevor, and Franklin—as they navigate a world of crime, corruption, and chaos. The game blends intense action, engaging storytelling, and an enormous open world filled with countless missions, side activities, and secrets to discover. Switch between characters seamlessly, each with their own skills and storylines, as you experience one of gaming's greatest narratives.

PLOT

Three criminals attempt to pull off a series of daring heists in Los Santos. Michael De Santa is a former bank robber in witness protection. Franklin Clinton is a young gang member seeking a way out. Trevor Phillips is an unpredictable drifter with a dark past. When their paths converge, they're drawn into an elaborate criminal conspiracy involving corrupt officials, rival gangs, and deadly cartels. Balancing their personal lives with their criminal enterprises, the trio must execute increasingly audacious heists while evading law enforcement and powerful enemies. Their choices will determine not only their fate but the fate of Los Santos itself.
