BATTLEFIELD V

OVERVIEW

Battlefield V is an all-out multiplayer warfare experience set during World War II. Experience large-scale, squad-based combat across a variety of iconic WWII locations. Choose your role, master teamwork, and dominate the battlefield with up to 64 players. The game emphasizes squad mechanics, destruction, and dynamic environments that change during matches. Every shot matters in this visceral, authentic portrayal of global warfare.

KEY FEATURES

⚔️ Squad-Based Combat

Work together with your squad for maximum effectiveness in large-scale battles

💥 Dynamic Destruction

Maps evolve as buildings collapse and environments shift during matches

🌍 WWII Locations

Fight across iconic European and Pacific theater battlefields

🎯 Multiple Game Modes

Conquest, Team Deathmatch, Combined Arms co-op, and more

PLOT

Battlefield V takes players back to the Human Drama of World War II. Through the eyes of various soldiers across different nations and fronts, experience untold stories of ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances. The War Stories campaign follows protagonists like an Under-The-Radar spy, a colonial soldier, and a tank crew as they navigate the moral complexities and human costs of warfare. Each story reveals a different perspective on WWII, emphasizing individual heroism and sacrifice during humanity's greatest conflict.
