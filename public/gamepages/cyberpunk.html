CYBERPUNK 2077

OVERVIEW

Cyberpunk 2077 is an immersive action-RPG set in the dystopian metropolis of Night City. You play as V, a mercenary customizable in appearance, abilities, and playstyle. Rise through the ranks of the futuristic underground while confronting a rogue AI that threatens your existence. Explore vast, detailed environments filled with quests, dangers, and opportunities. Choose your path: corpo, street kid, or nomad. Experience a narrative-driven world where your choices shape the outcome of multiple branching storylines.

PLOT

In the year 2077, the world is controlled by megacorporations. You're a street mercenary hired to steal a prototype of consciousness digitization technology. The job goes wrong when the data gets encoded into your neural implant alongside an AI of a legendary rockerboy terrorist. Now you have 6 months to live before the AI overwrites your consciousness. Navigate the neon-soaked streets, forge unlikely alliances, and uncover the truth behind the AI that's literally in your head. Your choices will determine whether you achieve immortality or face oblivion.
