LIMBO

LIMBO :

  • OVERVIEW

Game: Limbo

  • Developer: Playdead
  • Release Year: 2010
  • Platform: Xbox 360, PC, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, Mobile

Developed by a Small Indie Team

Limbo was developed by the Danish indie game studio Playdead. The development team was very small, with about 8 developers, and the game took around 6 years to complete.

A Story Without Dialogue

The game has no dialogue, no text explanations, and no narration. Players must understand the story through the environment and gameplay.

The Giant Spider Enemy

One of the most memorable parts of the game is the giant spider. The spider appears early in the game and creates intense puzzle and survival moments for players.

Searching for a Sister

In the game, the player controls a young boy searching for his sister in a strange and dangerous world. The meaning of the world and the ending is left open to interpretation.
