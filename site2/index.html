HOLLOW KNIGHT

HOLLOW KNIGHT :

  • OVERVIEW

Game: HOLLW KNIGHT

  • Developer: Team Cherry, an indie game studio from Adelaide, Australia.
  • Release Year: 2017
  • Platform: Xbox One, PC, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch

Made by a Very Small Team

The game was created by just three developers from the indie studio Team Cherry: Ari Gibson (art & design) William Pellen (design) David Kazi (programming) For such a massive and polished game, that’s incredibly small!

The Game Was Crowdfunded

Development was funded through a Kickstarter campaign in 2014, where the developers originally asked for about AU$35,000 and ended up raising over AU$57,000 from fans.

The Art Is Hand-Drawn

One of the most memorable parts of the game is the giant spider. The spider appears early in the game and creates intense puzzle and survival moments for players.

The Map System Was Inspired by Exploration Frustration

Early testers kept getting lost in the world of Hallownest, so the developers added the mapmaker character Cornifer to make navigation part of the gameplay experience.

A Sequel Is in Development

A sequel called Hollow Knight: Silksong was originally planned as DLC but became a full game because the scope got much bigger.
